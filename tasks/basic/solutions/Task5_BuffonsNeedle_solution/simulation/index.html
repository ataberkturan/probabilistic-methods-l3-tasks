Task 5 - Buffon's Needle Simulation

Sample space variables: x in [0, d/2], theta in [0, pi/2]

x x means the distance from the needle center to the nearest line. theta theta means the angle between the needle and the floor lines.

A hit happens when x <= (L/2) * sin(theta). In this version, use L <= d to keep the model simple.
